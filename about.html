Our Story

Built on trust since 2009

BrightSmile was founded by Dr. Sarah Mitchell, who after years of working in large hospital settings wanted to create a dental practice that truly put the patient first — not just clinically, but emotionally.

What started as a small two-chair practice has grown into a full-service clinic with a team of six specialists, covering everything from routine care to complex implant and orthodontic work.

We invest continuously in the latest dental technology — not because it's impressive, but because it means faster diagnoses, less discomfort, and better outcomes for every single patient.

Most importantly, we've built a culture of warmth, patience, and transparency. We believe every patient deserves a clear explanation of their options, fair pricing, and a team that genuinely cares about their health.
